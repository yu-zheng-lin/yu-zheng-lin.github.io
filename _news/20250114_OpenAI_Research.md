Yu-Zheng Lin, serving as Principal Investigator (PI), has been awarded acceptance into the OpenAI Researcher Access Program. The award provides $2000 in API credits to support research on large language model (LLM) stability, with applications to high-performance computing, digital twins, and cybersecurity.
